What is a Computer Network?
- A computer network is a group of devices connected together so they can share data, resources, and services.
- A computer network allows computers and devices to communicate with each other.

What is IP address?
- An IP address (Internet Protocol address) is a unique number assigned to every device on a network so it can send and receive data.
- An IP address is the digital address of a device on a network.
